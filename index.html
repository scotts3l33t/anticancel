Anti-Cancel

As American Citizens, we deserve to feel safe. Safe when we engage in
discourse, safe when we share our views, and safe from collateral damage when those around us speak.

What do we propose?

We propose that legislation be enacted to impose reasonable restrictions on Employers, Social Media and public forums, and Financial institutions to safeguard the American right to free speech and expression while ensuring Americans are able to live and provide for themselves without becoming a burden on the state.

Why is this important?

We have noticed a problem that has been gaining ground for a while now, at least beginning in the early 2000s. The problem is someone speaking out on something that they believe and a backlash forms that targets their employment, their relationships, and the financial institutions they are involved with. This causes many problems, problems for the person, yes, but also problems for the employers, public forums, and financial institutions targeted. It is our belief that removing the ability to censor people based on political beliefs is going to free them from having to spend time and money on things that do not improve their business. We also believe that it is forcing businesses into picking sides and surrendering potential customers based on political outlook.
